About FIPI IIT (ISM) SC


FIPI IIT ISM Student Chapter is the member organization of FIPI for Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad. Before the formation of FIPI, Petrotech ISM chapter was running successfully since 2007.  It is aimed at student's all round development and their direct interaction and relation with the Oil and Gas industry.

 The chapter keeps a special focus on the personal development of its members. JAM sessions, paper presentations, intern and placement talks and other such events are organised regularly to provide ample opportunities to the students to polish their presentations skills along with the knowledge of the industry.
